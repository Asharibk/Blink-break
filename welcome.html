Welcome to BlinkBreak

Your Pomodoro eye-care companion. Pin the extension to always see your break countdown in the toolbar.

📌 Pin BlinkBreak to your toolbar

1
Click the puzzle piece icon (Extensions) in the top-right of Chrome
2
Find BlinkBreak in the list
3
Click the pin icon next to it — it turns blue when pinned
4
You'll now see a 25m badge counting down to your next break
✅
You're all set!
Click the BlinkBreak icon in the toolbar
to start your first session.
